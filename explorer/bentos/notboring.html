FAQ answers, with attitude
Why does everything have a shadow?
Because light exists. Flat design was a cultural overcorrection — we stripped every ounce of dimension out of software and called it "modern." Shadows aren't decoration, they're depth cues your brain has been using since birth. We put them back.
Is this skeuomorphism?
Kind of — but not the leather-stitching iOS 6 variety. Think of it as a new species: not imitating real-world objects, but honoring the physics those objects obey. A button behaves like a button. A tile casts its shadow. A press feels like a press.
How do you make the buttons feel so good?
Six-layer shadow stack: inset top highlight, inset bottom shadow, outer ambient lift, outer drop, colored glow matching the hue, and a pressed-state compression that reduces outer shadows while deepening the inset bottom. Plus a matching haptic. Plus a sound.
Are the apps expensive?
We charge for craft. Our Studio subscription bundles Calculator, Weather, Habits, and Timers — fewer apps, more love in each one. Most apps help you save time. We help you savor it.
