Why does the gold appear in only one place per page?
Criterion Gold (#b4841e) is the institutional stamp — applied to the logo, primary CTA, and link underlines. Spreading it further would dilute its meaning. The teal hover (#1eaedb) is the only other chromatic moment, and it lives exclusively in transition.
