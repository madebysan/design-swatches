FAQ 1px black divider
What is Liquid Death?
Mountain water canned in infinitely recyclable tallboy cans. No plastic. No BS. Just water with a death metal attitude and a commitment to murdering your thirst.
Why are the cans shaped like beer cans?
Because beer cans are cooler — and aluminum is infinitely recyclable unlike plastic bottles. Also, it makes water socially acceptable at concerts.
Death to plastic — what does that mean?
Every can sold donates a portion of proceeds to help fight plastic pollution. The brand was built from day one around sustainability, just wrapped in a death-metal costume.
