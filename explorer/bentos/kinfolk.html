FAQ Hairline rules
Why is everything in serif and only one weight?
Kinfolk-Serif-Deck never goes heavier than weight 400. Hierarchy comes from scale, line-height, and uppercase — not boldness. The single 700 in the system lives in Kinfolk-Serif-Text for bylines. That's it.
