FAQ hairline divider
What is the Glossier design philosophy?
Flatness above all. No shadows, no gradients, no chrome. The system uses pure white canvases, a single pink surface (#f6e3e6), and one vermillion promo bar per page. Sharp 0px corners, 1px black borders, and Apercu for every single typographic role.
Why is the button gray, not pink?
Glossier Pink (#f6e3e6) is a surface — it lives on the footer and brand panels. Buttons are neutral #e8e8e8 so the product photography can dominate. Pink buttons would compete with packaging photography.
When does vermillion appear?
Once per page, in the site-wide promo bar only. It's the lipstick smudge — deliberately loud, deliberately singular. Using it anywhere else dilutes the brand moment.
