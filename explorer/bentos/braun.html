FAQ Hairline
Why are there no drop shadows?
Dieter Rams' principle: as little design as possible. Depth comes from hard-cut contrast between black and white sections, not from blurred elevation. The 128px pill radius and the single teal accent carry the full weight of affordance signaling.
Why pill 128px on every button?
