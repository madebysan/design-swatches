FAQ Dotted dividers
Why Monument Grotesk at only 16px?
Otherkind uses a single body size — no heading scale between the wordmark and the body. Sections are separated by space and mono labels, not by size. This creates the broadsheet-ledger feeling: everything is content, nothing shouts.
What are the two city clocks for?
The studio operates between Seoul and New York City. The clocks are not decorative — they tell you exactly what time it is for the people building the work.
Why is the orange used in only one place?
System Orange (#ff9500) is a quotation mark around the Mac Classic reference — applied to one decorative span inside the Photobook modal. Extending it to a brand color would break the discipline.
Elevation Tone, not shadow
Level 0 · Off-White Canvas · No Border
Level 1 · White Card · 1px Solid #000
Level 2 · Pixel Border · Outline Offset
No shadows anywhere. Tone contrast + 1px black borders do all the work.
