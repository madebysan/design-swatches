FAQ hairline divider · Magda uppercase
What does "made on demand" mean?
Every Le Labo fragrance is blended by hand at the moment of purchase, in the city where you buy it. Your name, the date, and the city are stamped onto the label — it exists nowhere until you ask for it.
Why stencil typography for a fragrance brand?
The Magda stencil face echoes the hand-stamped labels of the apothecary — visible bridges in the letterforms that read as real, printed, physical objects rather than digital type.
Is there a discovery set available?
Yes — a curated selection of travel-sized vials lets you explore the range before committing to a full bottle. Available in-store and online.
