What is Ivory?
Ivory is Tapbots' Mastodon client for iOS and macOS — the spiritual successor to Tweetbot, rebuilt from the ground up for the federated timeline. System requirements: iOS/iPadOS 16.1 or later, macOS 13 Ventura or later.
