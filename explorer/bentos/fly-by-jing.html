FAQ Publico body · JeanLuc Q
What makes Sichuan chili crisp different?
Sichuan chili crisp combines the numbing heat of Sichuan peppercorns with fragrant chilies, fermented black beans, and savory aromatics — a complex layering that transforms any dish from one-dimensional heat to full-spectrum flavor.
How spicy is the Zhong Sauce?
Zhong Sauce sits at a bold medium-high heat level — enough to feel the tingle, not enough to overpower the flavor. The numbing quality of Sichuan pepper means the heat is felt, not just burned.
Is Fly By Jing woman-owned?
Yes — Fly By Jing was founded by Jing Gao, a Chengdu-born chef and entrepreneur. Every product reflects her personal culinary philosophy and Sichuan heritage.
