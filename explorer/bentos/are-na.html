FAQ hairline dividers · serif answers
What is a channel?
A channel is a container for blocks — images, links, text, files, or other channels. It's the organizing unit of Are.na, equivalent to a folder that thinks for itself.
Who can see my blocks?
Channels can be public, private, or closed. Public channels are visible to anyone; private channels are visible only to you and collaborators you invite.
What does Premium include?
Premium members can add unlimited blocks and channels, access private channels, and support the Are.na community directly.
