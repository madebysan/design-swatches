What is a Link Kit?
A Link Kit is a single-page profile that lives at your personal URL — a curated collection of your best links, styled to match your aesthetic. Build it once, share it everywhere.
Can I upload my own photos?
Yes — each link card supports a full-bleed image or GIF. The system overlays a scrim gradient so your text stays legible over any photo.
How does the preview work?
The preview card mirrors your form in real time. It's sticky — it stays in view as you scroll the form so you always see exactly what your audience will see.
