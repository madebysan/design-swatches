FAQ hairline divider
What makes Erewhon's design system distinctive?
Silence is the signature. No accent colors, no gradients, no promotional chrome — structure emerges from two-percent tonal shifts between cream surfaces and generous whitespace. The dark ink header is the only chromatic frame.
Why only weights 400 and 500?
Bold weight is a shout. Erewhon communicates through tracking and restraint — heavily tracked uppercase at 0.10em to 0.16em does the work that bold weights do elsewhere, at a whisper volume.
Why is there no brand accent color?
Chromatic flatness is the luxury signal. The only non-neutral moment is the muted Sold Out sticker in #a23a3a — and even that reads as a printed paper tag, not an interactive UI element.
