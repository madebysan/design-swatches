FAQ details/summary
Is Asana free to use?

Asana's Basic plan is free for teams of up to 15 people and includes unlimited tasks, projects, messages, and activity log. Premium and Business plans unlock advanced features.

What integrations does Asana support?

Asana integrates with over 200 apps — Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Zoom, Jira, Salesforce, and many more through the Asana app directory.

Can I manage my team's workload in Asana?

Yes. Workload view (available on Premium and above) lets you see how much work each team member has and balance tasks to keep everyone at the right capacity.
