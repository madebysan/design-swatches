FAQ Hairline · Anton
How do I cancel my Sun Club membership?
Sign in to your account dashboard, head to subscriptions, and hit the cancel button. Your access continues until the end of the billing cycle.
Are breaking-news alerts free?
Yes. Breaking-news push alerts are free for all readers — Sun Club exclusives sit behind the gold flag.
Can I read The Sun in the US?
The Sun publishes a US edition out of New York with the same red-top contract. Switch editions from the masthead.
