FAQ details/summary
What are Allbirds made of?

Allbirds uses natural materials — merino wool, eucalyptus tree fiber, sugarcane, and recycled materials — to create footwear that's good for your feet and the planet.

Can I wash my Allbirds?

Yes. Wool Runners and Tree Runners are machine washable on a cold, gentle cycle. Air dry — never put them in the dryer.

What is SweetFoam?

SweetFoam is Allbirds' proprietary midsole material made from sugarcane — the world's first carbon-negative EVA foam, developed exclusively for Allbirds.
