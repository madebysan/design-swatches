Why Libre Baskerville everywhere?
One serif carries every headline, section title, body, and editorial pivot. The face's open apertures and humanist warmth do for a digital storefront what hand-painted signage does for a real trattoria — calm-confidence, family-restaurant authority, no aggressive bold weights.
What's the wine-warm orange glow about?
It's the brand's interactive thumbprint — `rgba(199, 91, 18, 0.4) 0 6px 16px` casts on hover so the gold pill doesn't just react, it warms up. Cool-blue shadows would break the trattoria contract.
When does cobalt blue appear?
Cobalt `#1976d2` is the Material-tinged inline link. Gold is the action contract; blue is the navigation contract. Mixing them collapses the hierarchy.
