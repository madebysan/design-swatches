Are the formulas truly historical?
Yes. The Officina holds the original handwritten recipe books in the Dominican convent's archive. Acqua della Regina, Aqua di Rose, and Acqua Antiflogistica all trace to formulas dated between 1381 and 1612, and continue to be produced from the same ingredients.
