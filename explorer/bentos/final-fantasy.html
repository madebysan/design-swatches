FAQ divider rhythm
Why two design registers?
The publisher portal is calm — paper white, Proxima Nova, sparse 4px radius. The in-game register is loud — Cinzel serif on cobalt, gold borders, 2px hard engraving shadows. Mixing them in one composition without a clear hand-off breaks both voices.
Where does the red appear?
Date stamps, the wordmark, form-field borders, and primary CTAs. 69 occurrences on the portal homepage. Hover steps to `#f71a27` — single-step brighter and more orange-leaning.
What's the radius scale?
4px buttons, 14px span chips, 32px feature CTAs, 50% modal closes. No 8px or 12px middle ground — the scale skips the comfortable middle on purpose.
