WHAT IS POOLSUITE?
POOLSUITE IS A COMMITTED BIT — A MUSIC STREAMING APP DISGUISED AS A 1995 MACINTOSH DESKTOP. THE DESIGN SYSTEM IS PIXEL FONTS, INSET BEVELS, AND A PEACHY-CREAM WALLPAPER. THE BIT IS THE BRAND.
WHY PIXEL FONTS?
PIXEL FONTS ONLY RENDER CRISP AT THEIR DESIGNED EM-SIZE. THE SYSTEM REFUSES TO DEVIATE FROM 10PX AND 16PX — RESCALING WOULD CORRUPT THE GRID.
WHAT IS THE BEVEL PAIR?
WHITE 1PX INSET TOP-LEFT + GRAY 1PX INSET BOTTOM-RIGHT. CANONICAL MAC OS SYSTEM 7 RAISED-BUTTON TREATMENT. NO GRADIENTS — EVER.
