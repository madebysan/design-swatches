What makes Olipop different from regular soda?
Olipop contains 9g of prebiotic fiber, botanical ingredients, and far less sugar than traditional sodas — all without sacrificing that fizzy, flavor-forward soda-fountain feeling you grew up loving.

We recommend starting with 1 can per day. Your gut microbiome is sensitive to change, and the prebiotic fiber does its best work with consistent, gradual introduction.
