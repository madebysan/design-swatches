FAQ 2px midnight divider · lowercase
what is recess, exactly?
Recess is a sparkling water blended with hemp extract and adaptogens — plant-based ingredients designed to help you feel calm and balanced without sedation.
will it make me feel sleepy?
Not at all. The adaptogens in Recess promote a relaxed focus — the opposite of jittery caffeine, without the drowsiness of a sleep aid.
how many should I drink per day?
One to two cans daily is the sweet spot for most people. Think of it as a ritual, not a remedy — a moment you build into your day.
