Independent Record Label · A Family of Artists

A label catalogue handed to an obsessive archivist instead of a brand agency. Futura uppercase masthead over OCR-B catalogue type, ink on uncoated paper, and the album artwork carrying every chromatic moment.

FAQ Hairline rules
Why no brand accent colour?
The album artwork carries every chromatic moment. Each LP cover is its own designed object — the surrounding chrome stays mute on purpose so the artist's work doesn't compete with a brand stamp.

Why is everything rectangular?
