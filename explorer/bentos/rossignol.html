How do I size Rossignol skis?
Use our sizing guide at the foot of each ski page. As a starting point, all-mountain skis sit between chin and forehead height for most resort skiers.
What's covered under the warranty?
Two-year manufacturer's warranty against defects in materials and craftsmanship. Wear from use and rock damage are not covered.
Do you ship to ski resorts?
Yes — we ship to most resort partners across the Alps, Rockies, and Hokkaido. Your order will be waiting at the rental shop.
