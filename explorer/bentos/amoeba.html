FAQ dotted dividers
Why dotted underlines instead of solid?
Every text link uses `border-bottom: 1px dotted #404040`, never `text-decoration: underline`. It's a typewriter-receipt cue that no other major retailer uses, and it's non-negotiable brand chrome — 23 instances on the homepage alone.
Why so much density?
2/3/4/5/7/10/11/13px values dominate. 2px is the most-used spacing value (67 occurrences). Modern luxuries — 24px gutters, 32px breathing room — break the flyer-wall personality.
What's the deal with the 6px corners?
Chunky enough to feel like a sleeve corner, sharp enough not to feel "designed." 45 instances across cards, buttons, and tabs. Tab panels use `0px 0px 6px 6px` for a sleeve-flap edge.
