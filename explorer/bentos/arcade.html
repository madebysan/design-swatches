FAQ
What is Arcade?
Arcade is a no-code demo creation tool. Capture flows in your browser, annotate them, and share as interactive walkthroughs.
Does it require installation?
Just install the Arcade Chrome extension. Recording, editing, and sharing all happen in the browser — no desktop app needed.
Can I embed demos in my website?
Yes. Every Arcade generates an embeddable iframe snippet you can drop into any site, Notion doc, or CMS.
